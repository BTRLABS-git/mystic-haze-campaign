The Story

A structured
composition.

A structured citrus composition built with layered green notes and mineral woods. Designed for everyday clarity.

The structure opens with the crisp, green precision of fig leaves, refined by the subtle clarity of white tea. Coconut adds a restrained softness, bridging the transition to a warm, enduring base of sandalwood, amber, and musk.

Each layer is measured to unfold deliberately, evoking the fresh, misted forest floor of an early morning — cool, humid, and textured. A modern, day-ready woody fragrance built to endure.

Photography Direction — Ryan Hing
Creator Selection Criteria
I. Tactile Silence Test

Can they handle stillness? Diegetic sound over hype music. Their work must appreciate breathing room — no fast cuts.

II. Environmental Sensitivity

They treat local urban landscapes — brutalist buildings, forests — as characters. Spatial storytelling, not backdrop.

III. Low-Light Sophistication

Mastery of shadow and texture. Their work must look intentional in the dark. We want a filmic, accentuating grade.

IV. Casting: Character Over Symmetry

Avoid catalogue beauty. Faces with narrative weight — people who look like they have an internal monologue.
